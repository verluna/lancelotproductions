The Path to Profitability

A 4-step plan to transform the account from a traffic-generation tool into a powerful, revenue-driving engine.

1

Implement Conversion Tracking

Install the Meta Pixel on the final ticket purchase confirmation page. This is the non-negotiable first step to measuring success.

2

Relaunch with a Sales Objective

Pause "Link Click" campaigns and create new "Sales" campaigns optimizing for "Purchases" to align ad spend with revenue goals.

3

Consolidate & Scale Audiences

Shift from single-city ad sets to broader regional campaigns. Focus budget on winning demographics (Females, 25-44) to improve efficiency.

4

Develop a Retargeting Funnel

Create campaigns to re-engage website visitors who haven't purchased. This recaptures high-intent users and boosts overall sales.
